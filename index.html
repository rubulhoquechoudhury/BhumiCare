From soil to insight in four simple steps.

We handle the complexity of IoT hardware, connectivity, and models, so you can focus on growing.

1

Place sensors in your fields

Our team helps you plan sensor locations and installs the BhumiCare outdoor units directly in your soil, configured for your crops and geography.

2

Stream live soil data to the cloud

Devices securely transmit high-resolution readings via LTE or LoRaWAN. BhumiCloud™ cleans and enriches the data automatically.

3

AI models generate recommendations

Our agronomy-trained models map soil profiles against crop requirements and best-practice playbooks to generate guidance.

4

You act with confidence

Access the BhumiCare recommendation dashboard on web, Android, iOS, or feature keypad phones to see what to add to the soil, when to irrigate, and which crops are best suited to your current land condition.

Frequently asked questions

Have more questions? Reach out and we will be happy to walk you through pilots and pricing for your fields.

Where can BhumiCare sensors be installed?

BhumiCare outdoor units can be installed in open fields, polyhouses, orchards, and trial plots. Our team will help you design an installation plan tailored to your soil type and crop mix.

Do I need constant internet connectivity on the farm?

Sensors use built‑in LTE or LoRaWAN where available and buffer data if connectivity drops. The dashboard automatically updates once the connection is restored.

Which crops are currently supported?

Our crop models currently cover key staples such as wheat, rice, maize, soybean, cotton, and potatoes, with more being added based on partner demand.

How do pilots and pricing work?

We typically start with a limited-acre pilot to prove value within one season. Pricing depends on the number of sensors, fields, and the level of support required.
